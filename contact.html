Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I donate to Hope Smile Foundation?

You can donate through multiple methods: bank transfer, mobile money (MTN, AirtelTigo, Vodafone), PayPal, or by visiting our office. Visit our Donate page for detailed instructions.

How do I volunteer with Hope Smile Foundation?

We welcome volunteers! Please fill out our volunteer application form or contact our Volunteer Coordinator, Amina Diallo, at volunteer@kidhope.org. We have opportunities in education, fundraising, events, and administrative support.

What percentage of donations goes directly to programs?

98% of all donations go directly to our programs supporting children. Only 2% is allocated to administrative costs. We are committed to transparency and provide annual financial reports on our website.

Can I sponsor a specific child?

Yes, we offer child sponsorship programs. Sponsors receive updates, photos, and progress reports about the child they support. Contact our sponsorship department at sponsorship@kidhope.org for more information.
